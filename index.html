Our Heritage

Since 1907 • Serving UoE Since 2003

"The Scout Movement is a game for boys and girls under the leadership of adults, based on the Scout Promise and Law, and aimed at developing young people physically, intellectually, socially, and spiritually."

— Lord Baden-Powell

🎯

Mission

To develop young people into responsible citizens and leaders through outdoor adventure and community service.

👁️

Vision

To be the leading youth movement in Kenya, creating a better world through education and empowerment.

⚜️

Promise

"On my honor, I promise to do my best, to do my duty to God and my country, to help other people at all times..."

The Scout Trail

Adventure • Skills • Service

1

🏕️ Camping & Survival

Master the art of outdoor living. Weekend expeditions, bushcraft skills, orienteering, and survival training in Kenya's diverse landscapes.

2

🩺 First Aid & Safety

Comprehensive first aid certification, emergency response training, and disaster preparedness. Be ready to save lives.

3

🌱 Environmental Conservation

Tree planting initiatives, plastic tide campaigns, and wildlife conservation projects. Protecting our planet for future generations.

4

👥 Community Service

Blood donation drives, school outreach programs, and humanitarian projects. Making a tangible difference in our community.

5

🎖️ Leadership Development

Public speaking, project management, team building, and mentorship programs. Forge the leaders of tomorrow.
