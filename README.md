## 🌲 About the Project

This is the official digital archive and recruitment platform for the **University of Eldoret ELITE CREW Scouts Movement**, featuring the memorable **Moiben Sub Counties Camping Trip 2026**. The website combines rugged outdoor aesthetics with modern web technologies to create an immersive scouting experience.

### 🎯 Mission
To digitally preserve the spirit of scouting while recruiting new members into the ELITE CREW family through an engaging, adventure-themed web experience.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎨 **Immersive Design**
- **Rope & Knot Borders** - Authentic scout-camp aesthetics with animated rope textures
- **Compass Cursor** - Interactive compass that follows your journey across the site
- **Campfire Loading Screen** - Animated tent opening sequence with flickering flames
- **Scrapbook Gallery** - Polaroid-style photo collection with push pins and rotation effects
- **Trail Map Navigation** - Interactive checkpoint system showing the scout journey

### 📸 **Moiben 2026 Gallery**
13 curated moments from the camping trip:
1. 🏕️ Setting up camp at Moiben
2. 👥 Sub-county patrols assembly
3. 📚 Leadership training session
4. 🎯 Scout skills demonstration
5. 🥾 Hiking the Moiben trails
6. 🔥 Campfire stories evening
7. ➰ Knot tying competition
8. 🌅 Morning drill and exercises
9. 🌿 Survival skills training
10. 🌱 Tree planting initiative
11. 📋 Patrol leaders briefing
12. 🏆 Award ceremony
13. 👋 The Elite Crew - Group Photo

### ⚡ **Interactive Elements**
- **GSAP Animations** - Smooth scroll-triggered animations throughout
- **Parallax Scrolling** - Depth effects on hero section and background elements
- **Animated Fireflies** - Ambient particle effects in the hero section
- **Progress Bar** - Scout-themed rank progression indicator
- **Lightbox Viewer** - Enhanced photo viewing with vintage borders

### 📱 **Responsive Design**
- Mobile-first approach with breakpoint optimizations
- Touch-friendly navigation for trail activities
- Adaptive scrapbook layout for all screen sizes
